## ðŸŽ¯ HR Best Practices

### Job Descriptions
- **Clear Expectations**: Define success metrics
- **Inclusive Language**: Avoid biased terms
- **Realistic Requirements**: Distinguish must-have vs nice-to-have
- **Transparent Compensation**: Include salary ranges where possible
- **Growth Path**: Show career progression opportunities

### Performance Reviews
- **Regular Feedback**: Don't wait for annual reviews
- **Specific Examples**: Use concrete incidents
- **Balanced Perspective**: Include strengths and areas for growth
- **Actionable Goals**: Set SMART objectives
- **Two-Way Dialogue**: Encourage employee input

### Compensation Planning
- **Market Data**: Use industry benchmarks
- **Internal Equity**: Ensure fairness across teams
- **Performance Link**: Tie increases to results
- **Transparency**: Explain compensation philosophy

### Employee Development
- **Individual Development Plans**: Personalized growth paths
- **Continuous Learning**: Support training and conferences
- **Mentorship Programs**: Pair junior and senior employees
- **Stretch Assignments**: Provide growth opportunities

## ðŸ”‘ Key HR Metrics

### Hiring Metrics
- **Time to Hire**: Days from posting to offer acceptance
- **Quality of Hire**: Performance ratings of new hires
- **Source of Hire**: Which channels produce best candidates
- **Offer Acceptance Rate**: % of offers accepted
- **Diversity Metrics**: Representation across demographics

### Retention Metrics
- **Turnover Rate**: % of employees leaving
- **Retention by Tenure**: Breakout by years of service
- **Exit Reasons**: Why people leave
- **Cost of Turnover**: Financial impact
- **High Performer Retention**: Critical talent retention

### Engagement Metrics
- **eNPS**: Employee Net Promoter Score
- **Survey Participation**: % completing engagement surveys
- **Development Plan Completion**: % with active IDPs
- **Internal Mobility**: % moving to new roles internally
