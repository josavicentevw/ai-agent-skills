## üìä Performance Review Framework

### Rating Scale

| Rating | Description | Distribution Target |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| **5 - Exceptional** | Consistently exceeds expectations; extraordinary impact | 5-10% |
| **4 - Exceeds** | Frequently exceeds expectations; significant impact | 20-30% |
| **3 - Meets** | Consistently meets expectations; solid performer | 50-60% |
| **2 - Needs Improvement** | Sometimes meets expectations; requires support | 5-10% |
| **1 - Unsatisfactory** | Rarely meets expectations; performance issues | <5% |

### Competency Areas

1. **Technical Excellence** (35%)
   - Code quality and architecture
   - Technical problem-solving
   - System design capabilities

2. **Delivery & Execution** (25%)
   - Project completion
   - Meeting deadlines
   - Quality of deliverables

3. **Collaboration & Communication** (20%)
   - Cross-team collaboration
   - Communication clarity
   - Stakeholder management

4. **Leadership & Influence** (15%)
   - Mentorship
   - Technical leadership
   - Raising team standards

5. **Growth & Learning** (5%)
   - Continuous improvement
   - Skill development
   - Adaptability
