## üîß Content Optimization Strategy

### Quarterly Content Audits

**Review All Content:**
1. **Traffic Performance:** Identify declining articles
2. **Ranking Changes:** Monitor keyword movements
3. **Conversion Rate:** Find low-converting high-traffic pages

**Optimization Actions:**
- **High Traffic + Low Conversion:** Improve CTAs, add lead magnets
- **Good Content + Poor Ranking:** Enhance SEO, build backlinks
