## ðŸŽ¯ Marketing Best Practices

### Product Marketing
- **Know Your ICP:** Define ideal customer profile precisely
- **Differentiation:** Clear unique value proposition
- **Positioning:** Own a specific category or use case
- **Message Testing:** Validate messaging with customers
- **Launch Tiers:** Not all launches are equal (T1/T2/T3)

### Content Marketing
- **Quality Over Quantity:** Better to publish 1 great piece than 10 mediocre
- **SEO Foundation:** Keyword research drives strategy
- **Distribution is Key:** Spend 20% creating, 80% promoting
- **Repurpose Content:** One webinar = blog + social + email + podcast
- **Measure What Matters:** Focus on business impact, not vanity metrics

### Growth Marketing
- **Test Everything:** Run experiments continuously
- **Focus on Retention:** It's cheaper than acquisition
- **Product-Led Growth:** Let product drive acquisition
- **Referral Programs:** Leverage existing customers
- **Data-Driven:** Make decisions based on metrics

### Demand Generation
- **Full-Funnel Approach:** TOFU, MOFU, BOFU content
- **Multi-Touch Attribution:** Understand the journey
- **Lead Scoring:** Prioritize sales-ready leads
- **Nurture Campaigns:** Most leads aren't ready to buy immediately
- **Sales Alignment:** Marketing and sales must work together

## ðŸŽ¨ Marketing Channels Guide

### Paid Channels
**Search Engine Marketing (SEM)**
- Best for: High-intent keywords, bottom-funnel
- Typical CPC: $2-$50+ depending on industry
- Measurement: CPA, ROAS, conversion rate

**Display Advertising**
- Best for: Brand awareness, remarketing
- Typical CPM: $1-$10
- Measurement: Impressions, CTR, view-through conversions

**Paid Social**
- Best for: Targeted awareness, lead gen
- Platforms: LinkedIn (B2B), Facebook/Instagram (B2C)
- Typical CPC: $3-$15 (LinkedIn), $0.50-$3 (Facebook)
- Measurement: Engagement, leads, CAC

**Content Syndication**
- Best for: Thought leadership, lead gen
- Platforms: Demand Gen platforms
- Typical CPL: $50-$200
- Measurement: MQL quality, SQL conversion

### Organic Channels
**SEO & Content**
- Best for: Long-term traffic growth, thought leadership
- Timeline: 6-12 months to see results
- Measurement: Organic traffic, rankings, conversions

**Social Media**
- Best for: Community building, engagement
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube
- Measurement: Followers, engagement rate, traffic

**Email Marketing**
- Best for: Nurturing, retention, upsells
- Typical OR: 20-30%, CTR: 2-5%
- Measurement: Opens, clicks, conversions

**Community & Forums**
- Best for: User engagement, support, advocacy
- Platforms: Slack, Discord, Reddit, Product Hunt
- Measurement: Active users, engagement, NPS

## ðŸ“ˆ Marketing Metrics Dictionary

### Awareness Metrics
- **Impressions:** Number of times content is displayed
- **Reach:** Unique individuals who see content
- **Brand Awareness:** % of target market aware of brand
- **Share of Voice:** Brand mentions vs competitors

### Engagement Metrics
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Clicks / Impressions
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- **Time on Page:** Average time spent on content
- **Bounce Rate:** % who leave after viewing one page

### Conversion Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** Conversions / Visitors
- **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** Spend / Leads
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CAC):** Spend / Customers
- **Lead to Customer Rate:** Customers / Leads

### Revenue Metrics
- **Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL):** Leads meeting criteria
- **Sales Qualified Leads (SQL):** Leads accepted by sales
- **Pipeline Generated:** Dollar value of opportunities
- **Revenue Attribution:** Revenue influenced by marketing
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue / Ad Spend
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** Total revenue per customer

### Efficiency Metrics
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** Should be >3:1
- **Payback Period:** Time to recover CAC
- **Marketing % of Revenue:** Spend / Revenue (typically 10-20%)
- **Sales Cycle Length:** Days from lead to customer

## ðŸŽ¯ Campaign Planning Checklist

### Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)
- [ ] Define campaign goals and KPIs
- [ ] Identify target audience and personas
- [ ] Develop positioning and key messages
- [ ] Create asset list and timeline
- [ ] Set budget and channel allocation
- [ ] Assign roles and responsibilities
- [ ] Build landing pages and forms
- [ ] Set up tracking and analytics
- [ ] Prepare sales enablement materials
- [ ] Schedule content and ads

### Launch Week
- [ ] Activate paid campaigns
- [ ] Publish blog posts and content
- [ ] Send email announcements
- [ ] Post on social media
- [ ] Engage with comments and questions
- [ ] Monitor metrics and performance
- [ ] Adjust based on early data

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)
- [ ] Daily performance monitoring
- [ ] Weekly optimization (ads, landing pages)
- [ ] Lead follow-up and nurturing
- [ ] Content performance analysis
- [ ] Budget reallocation based on performance
- [ ] Stakeholder reporting
- [ ] Document learnings for next campaign

2. **Feature Adoption:** Drive usage of key features
3. **Email Nurturing:** Regular value delivery
4. **Community Building:** Connect users

1. **Upsell Campaigns:** Promote premium features
2. **Usage-Based Triggers:** Offer upgrades at limits
3. **Annual Plans:** Discount for commitment
4. **Add-Ons:** Complementary products
5. **Enterprise Sales:** High-touch for large deals

### Referral
1. **Referral Programs:** Reward both parties
2. **Customer Stories:** Case studies and testimonials

4. **Review Sites:** G2, Capterra presence
